Steak

Description

This steak recipe is delicious. There is a reason why I love it so much.

This recipe has been in my family for years, it is truly special and is a sure way to make the best steak.

Ingredients

  • Grassfed Ribeye Steak (1x)
  • Rosemary
  • Thyme
  • Ghee
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  1. Pat dry Ribeye, add salt on both sides and let it sit at room temperature for 40 minutes.
  2. Put a cast iron pan on a hot stove. Add half a tsp of Ghee once the pan is hot.
  3. Put Steak on and leave it for 2 minutes 45 seconds then flip. Leave it for another 2 minutes 45 seconds.
  4. Sear the sides for 45 seconds.
  5. Turn heat off and add it a large chunk of butter, rosemary, and thyme. Butter base steak for a minute.
  6. Pepper both sides of steak and let it sit for 3 minutes before eating.
