Eggs

Description

This sunny side up eggs recipe is delicious. There is a reason why I love it so much.

This recipe has been in my family for years, it is truly special and is a sure way to make the best eggs.

Ingredients

  • Pasture Raised Eggs
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  1. Put a cast iron pan on low heat, add a large amount of butter once the pan is warm.
  2. Crack eggs directly into pan. Ensure no shell gets in pan.
  3. Put a lid over the pan to keep heat in pan so the top gets cooked, there will be no need to flip the eggs.
  4. After 3 or so minutes, the whites of the eggs should be bubbling and solidly together, it is ready to be salt, peppered, and served.
